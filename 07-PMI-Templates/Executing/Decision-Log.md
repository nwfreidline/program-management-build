## Instructions

1. **Log all significant decisions** — anything that affects baselines, direction, or stakeholders
2. **Document at time of decision** — don't rely on memory; capture in the meeting
3. **Include alternatives** — future reviewers need to understand why other options were rejected
4. **State rationale clearly** — this is the most valuable part of the record
5. **Review quarterly** — ensure decisions are still valid as context changes
6. **Supersede, don't delete** — if a decision is reversed, mark as "Superseded" and reference the new decision
