## Response Strategy Definitions

| Strategy | Type | Definition |
|----------|------|-----------|
| **Avoid** | Threat | Eliminate the threat by changing the plan |
| **Mitigate** | Threat | Reduce probability or impact to acceptable threshold |
| **Transfer** | Threat | Shift impact to a third party (insurance, contract) |
| **Accept** | Threat | Acknowledge and take no proactive action |
| **Exploit** | Opportunity | Ensure the opportunity is realized |
| **Enhance** | Opportunity | Increase probability or impact |
| **Share** | Opportunity | Allocate ownership to a third party best positioned to capture |
