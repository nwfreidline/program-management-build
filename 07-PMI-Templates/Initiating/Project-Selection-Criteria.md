### Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Strategic Alignment | 25% | How well does it support organizational goals? |
| Financial Return | 20% | ROI, NPV, payback period |
| Risk Level | 15% | Probability and impact of failure |
| Resource Availability | 15% | Do we have the people and tools? |
| Urgency | 15% | Time-sensitivity, regulatory deadlines |
| Stakeholder Support | 10% | Level of organizational buy-in |

*Scale: 1 (Low) to 5 (High)*

### Weighted Score Calculation

`Weighted Score = Σ (Score × Weight)`

Example: If Project A scores 4 on Strategic Alignment:
`4 × 0.25 = 1.00` (contribution from that criterion)
