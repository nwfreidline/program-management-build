# Layer 6: Career Growth

> A methodology for capturing daily work in real time and converting it into STAR-format accomplishments for performance reviews, promotions, and interviews.

---

## The Problem

Every 6-12 months, you're asked: "What did you accomplish?"

Most people scramble to reconstruct their work from memory, emails, and calendar entries. The result is incomplete, undersells your impact, and takes hours to compile.

---

## The Solution: Capture → Convert → Export

```
Daily work (checkboxes in Projects.md)
        ↓ (automatic via sync)
Completed projects (To Growth Tracker.md)
        ↓ (monthly review)
STAR-format entries (Career Tracker app)
        ↓ (on demand)
Exports (Word/Excel/Markdown for reviews)
```

### Step 1: Capture (Continuous)
You're already doing this if you use Layer 1. Every time you check `[x]` in `Projects.md`, you're building your evidence trail.

### Step 2: Accumulate (Automatic)
When a project completes, move it to `To Growth Tracker.md`. The kanban board marks it "Done." Your timeline of delivered work grows automatically.

### Step 3: Convert (Monthly)
Once a month, open the Career Tracker app and convert your recent completions into STAR entries:
- **Situation** — What problem or gap existed?
- **Task** — What were you responsible for?
- **Action** — What did you do? (auto-converted to past tense)
- **Result** — What was the measurable outcome?

### Step 4: Export (On Demand)
When review time comes, export your STAR entries to Word/Excel/Markdown. Your promotion case writes itself.

---

## The STAR Framework

| Element | Question to Answer | Example |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| **Situation** | What was the context or challenge? | "The team spent 3 hours/week manually tracking 200+ maintenance work orders across Excel spreadsheets" |
| **Task** | What was your specific role? | "I was responsible for designing and implementing an automated tracking solution" |
| **Action** | What did you do? | "Built a Python pipeline that pulls data from the maintenance system, calculates compliance windows, and generates scheduled calendars automatically" |
| **Result** | What was the measurable outcome? | "Reduced schedule generation from 3 hours to 5 minutes per week. Zero missed compliance windows in 6 months." |

## Career Tracker App

The Career Tracker app (included in Layer 3) provides:

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| STAR entry creation | Guided form with auto-past-tense conversion |
| Import from file | Pull in items from Markdown, Word, Excel, or text files |
| Multi-format export | Generate Word, Excel, Markdown, or plain text outputs |
| Data protection | Versioning, entry locks, and audit trail |
| Search and filter | Find entries by title, date, or keyword |

### Usage

1. Open Career Tracker (double-click `Career Tracker.pyw`)
2. Click "Import Items" → paste items from your `To Growth Tracker.md`
3. Click each imported item → fill in STAR fields
4. When review time comes → Export to your preferred format

## Growth Timeline Template

For a long-term career view, maintain a growth timeline that summarizes your delivered work by month:

```markdown
# Growth Timeline — 2026

## January 2026
- Delivered automated budget calculation pipeline (reduced 2-week process to 2 hours)
- Launched vendor feedback reporting mechanism

## February 2026
- Completed PMP certification exam
- Built interview preparation tool for team (372 questions, 16 categories)

## March 2026
- Deployed maintenance schedule automation (MS Project XML generation)
- Led team training on new procurement process (12 attendees)

...
```

This timeline becomes your master evidence document. Each line can expand into a full STAR entry when needed.

## Obsidian Tracking Guide

For the daily tracking workflow within Obsidian:

### Minimum Viable Tracking
1. When you finish a task, check the box: `- [x] Task description`
2. When a project is fully done, move it to `To Growth Tracker.md`
3. That's it. Two actions. Everything else is automated or done monthly.

### Weekly Review (5 minutes)
Every Friday, scan your `Projects.md`:
- Are all completed items checked off?
- Any new tasks to add?
- Any projects that should move to "Done"?

### Monthly Conversion (30 minutes)
First Monday of each month:
1. Review items in `To Growth Tracker.md`
2. Open Career Tracker app
3. Convert each completed project to a STAR entry
4. Add to your Growth Timeline

## Tips

- **Quantify everything.** "Improved process" is weak. "Reduced cycle time from 5 days to 4 hours" is strong.
- **Action verbs matter.** Start each action with a strong verb: built, designed, led, implemented, automated, reduced, delivered.
- **One entry per project.** Don't split a project into multiple STAR entries unless it genuinely had distinct deliverables.
- **Include the "so what."** Every Result should answer: "Why should anyone care?"
- **Past tense by default.** The Career Tracker auto-converts, but if writing manually, always use past tense for completed work.
