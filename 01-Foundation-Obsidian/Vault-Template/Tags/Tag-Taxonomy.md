# Tag Taxonomy

> Standardized tags for consistent categorization across the vault.

---

## Status Tags
- `#active` ‚Äî Currently being worked
- `#blocked` ‚Äî Waiting on external dependency
- `#deferred` ‚Äî Pushed to a future date
- `#complete` ‚Äî Finished (used in notes, not Projects.md)

## Priority Tags
- `#p1` ‚Äî Critical / immediate
- `#p2` ‚Äî High / this week
- `#p3` ‚Äî Medium / this month
- `#p4` ‚Äî Low / backlog

## Category Tags
- `#project` ‚Äî Multi-phase initiative
- `#task` ‚Äî Single action item
- `#program` ‚Äî Recurring operational program
- `#meeting` ‚Äî Meeting-related note

## Domain Tags (Customize These)
- `#vendor` ‚Äî Vendor management
- `#procurement` ‚Äî Purchasing and contracts
- `#compliance` ‚Äî Regulatory or policy compliance
- `#budget` ‚Äî Financial planning and tracking
- `#maintenance` ‚Äî Preventative or corrective maintenance
- `#training` ‚Äî Team development and training
- `#process` ‚Äî Process improvement

## People Tags (Optional)
- `#team/[name]` ‚Äî Items assigned to a specific person
- `#stakeholder/[name]` ‚Äî Items involving a stakeholder
